Structure Group Session• Opening &amp short channel for anything that may come up• Ask participants to do a short channelling on their issue and explain as I go how I do a little bit of the questioning and find the bottom of the issue until it’s unlocked and guidance about what homework the participant needs to do in their own practice• Do short Q/A through the chat during the whole process• Self realisation channelling about the subjects that came up in the short reading stage• Give prompts for self enquiry, journaling, and meditations that are relevant• Group meditation to drop in and receive physical and emotional healing and completion• ClosingDuration -&gt 1:30h-2h
